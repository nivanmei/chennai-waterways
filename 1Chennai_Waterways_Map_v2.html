Critical Chokepoints — Click marker on map
⚠️
Marina Beach — Cooum Mouth
13.0610°N 80.2990°E · Seasonal sandbar 200–400m wide. 1m drop / 20km = stagnation. Tidal backwash blocks 6–8h/tide. Needs tidal gate.
⚠️
Adyar Estuary
13.0012°N 80.2990°E · 2015: 348mm, 400+ deaths. Sandbar + seawater intrusion 3–5km inland. Chembarambakkam gate discharges trigger flash floods.
🏭
Ennore Creek / Kosasthalaiyar Mouth
13.2312°N 80.3225°E · Ocean discharge mouth. 200–250 MLD industrial wastewater from Ennore zone. Only 40–50% treatment compliance. Heavy metals Cr, Ni, Cd.
🚧
Buckingham ⊥ Cooum Junction
13.0725°N 80.2832°E · 60% of untreated sewage. 400–700 informal outfalls. 5,000+ encroachments. HC 2022 order unmet.
💧
Chembarambakkam Lake Outlet
13.0075°N 80.0125°E · Primary Reservoir Spillway Gates. 3.65 TMC Capacity / 2.59 TMC Storage. Gate releases cause Adyar flooding.
🏘️
Cooum Hutment Belt
13.0688°N 80.2450°E · 3,500+ illegal structures. Prevents dredging. Narrows channel 15–25%. 8–12 MLD direct discharge. 87-yr clearance at current pace.
🔴
Southern Canal Squeeze (Thiruvanmiyur–Palavakkam) CHOKED / RISK ZONE
12.9550°N 80.2550°E · 5.5 km critical urban squeeze. Bed capacity reduced >65% by siltation. Tidal lockages, raw domestic sewage outfalls choke flow velocity. Monsoon backwater flooding risk across ECR-OMR corridors.
